Spread = Charge Rate − Pay Rate, less statutory oncosts · June 2026

Calculation logic (BRD §7.6.2)

Client Cost = Hours × Charge Rate
Agency Cost = Hours × Pay Rate
Spread = Client Cost − Agency Cost

Net Margin = Spread − (Employer's NI + WTR + Pension + Apprenticeship Levy). Abstract's remuneration is the spread and its statutory breakdown — never a percentage of margin.
